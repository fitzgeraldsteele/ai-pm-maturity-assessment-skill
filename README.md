# AI PM Maturity Assessment

A Claude Code skill for benchmarking how effectively a product management team uses AI across the full product lifecycle.

## What It Does

Produces an honest, evidence-based assessment of a PM team's AI maturity. Scores 12 PM lifecycle phases on a 0–5 scale, identifies strengths and gaps, and generates concrete next steps — grounded in real workspace artifacts, not self-reported estimates.

## The 12 Lifecycle Phases

1. Product Discovery & Market Research
2. Analyzing Customer Feedback & User Research
3. Competitive Analysis & Market Intelligence
4. Requirements Gathering & PRD Writing
5. Metrics Definition & Success Criteria
6. Design & Prototyping
7. Stakeholder Alignment & Communication
8. Developer Communication & Technical Specs
9. Building, Coding & Implementation
10. Testing, QA & AI Evals
11. Launch, Growth & Distribution
12. Team Structure, Hiring & AI Adoption

## Scoring Scale

| Score | Label | Definition |
|:-----:|-------|------------|
| 0 | Not Started | No AI in this area |
| 1 | Experimented | Ad-hoc or one-off use |
| 2 | Emerging | Some tooling, not yet systematic |
| 3 | Established | Repeatable workflows; regular use |
| 4 | Advanced | AI-native with feedback loops |
| 5 | Best Practice | Full coverage; team-wide; evolving |

## Files

- `ai-pm-maturity-assessment/SKILL.md` — The skill definition, rubric, assessment workflow, and output format
- `AI in Product Management — Lifecycle Rubric & Benchmark Guide.md` — Full rubric with sourcing and best-practice signals for each phase

## Usage

Install the skill in a Claude Code workspace and invoke it when you want to:

- Benchmark a PM team's AI adoption
- Identify the highest-leverage gaps in AI-assisted workflows
- Plan where to invest in AI tooling for a product team
- Compare practices against industry benchmarks from leading AI-native companies

## Sources

The rubric synthesizes practices from 40+ episodes of Lenny's Podcast (Jan 2025–Apr 2026), drawing from practitioners at Anthropic, OpenAI, Microsoft, LinkedIn, Airtable, Vercel, Reforge, Figma, Intercom, and others.
